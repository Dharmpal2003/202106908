AWM Rifle

AWM

The AWM (Arctic Warfare Magnum) is a bolt-action sniper rifle manufactured by Accuracy International. It is renowned for its accuracy, reliability, and long-range capabilities, making it a preferred choice among military and law enforcement agencies, as well as civilian long-range shooting enthusiasts.

Key features and specifications of the AWM include:

  • Caliber: .300 Winchester Magnum, .338 Lapua Magnum, .300 Norma Magnum
  • Barrel Length: Varies depending on caliber and configuration
  • Construction: Robust and durable materials, typically featuring a steel receiver and aluminum chassis
  • Action: Bolt-action mechanism with a smooth and precise operation
  • Accuracy: Sub-MOA (Minute of Angle) accuracy out to extended ranges
  • Optics: Typically equipped with high-quality telescopic sights for precision shooting
  • Magazine Capacity: Typically comes with a detachable box magazine with various capacities
  • Accessories: Compatible with a wide range of aftermarket accessories, including bipods, suppressors, and optics mounts
  • Usage: Suitable for long-range target shooting, hunting, and military/law enforcement sniping applications
