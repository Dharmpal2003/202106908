CZ Bren 2Ms

The CZ Bren 2Ms is a modern, modular assault rifle designed and manufactured by Česká zbrojovka Uherský Brod (CZUB) in the Czech Republic.

It is the latest iteration of the CZ Bren series and is known for its compact design, lightweight construction, and versatility. The Bren 2Ms is chambered in various calibers, including 5.56x45mm NATO and 7.62x39mm, offering users the flexibility to choose their preferred cartridge for different applications.

Key features:

  • Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO, 7.62x39mm (other calibers may be available)
  • Action: Gas-operated, selective-fire (semi-automatic and full-automatic)
  • Barrel Length: 11 to 14 inches (may vary depending on model and configuration)
  • Weight: Approximately 6 to 7 pounds (may vary depending on model and configuration)
  • Stock: Adjustable folding stock with multiple length-of-pull positions
  • Handguard: Modular aluminum handguard with Picatinny rails for attaching accessories
  • Sights: Flip-up iron sights or compatibility with various optical sights
  • Magazine Capacity: Standard capacity varies depending on caliber and magazine type
