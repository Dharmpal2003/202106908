Man-Made Barrett M82 Sniper Rifle

The Man-Made Barrett M82 Sniper Rifle is a precision long-range rifle known for its power, accuracy, and reliability. It is commonly used by military and law enforcement units, as well as civilian shooters, for various applications including sniping, marksmanship training, and long-range target shooting.

Key features:

  • Caliber: .50 BMG (12.7x99mm NATO)
  • Action: Semi-automatic, recoil-operated
  • Barrel Length: 20 to 29 inches (may vary depending on model and configuration)
  • Weight: Approximately 30 to 35 pounds (may vary depending on model and configuration)
  • Stock: Fixed or adjustable stock options available
  • Optics: Typically equipped with high-power telescopic sights for long-range shooting
  • Magazine Capacity: 10-round detachable box magazine
  • Accuracy: Capable of sub-MOA (Minute of Angle) accuracy at extended ranges
