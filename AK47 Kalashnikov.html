About AK47 Kalashnikov

The PSAK-47 GF3 was designed from the ground up to be a new standard in AK-47 rifles, utilizing all new precision manufactured parts. Thoroughly tested in development, we tortured tested to 10,000 rounds to ensure a quality product. Not to be satisfied with just a good AK-47, we kept improving and now are proud to announce our PSAK-47 Gen3 Hammer Forged (GF3) rifle with a hammer forged bolt, carrier, and front trunnion.

The AK103 was one of the last designs overseen directly by Mikhail Kalashnikov within the iconic AK platform. Chambered in 7.62x39 the AK103 returns to its roots and provides the consumer with the traditional round chosen by the Russian Army for decades. Based on the AK74 the 103 will accept all AK74AKM furniture and accessories. Features a forged Carrier 5.5 mm forged trunnion chrome lined gas system cleaning rod and side optic rail. Made in the USA.

Additional features include:

  • ALG Trigger
  • JL Billet Rail
  • Forged "Moekov" design
