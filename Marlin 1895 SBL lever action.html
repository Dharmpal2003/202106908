Marlin 1895 SBL lever action Rifle

Marlin 1895 SBL Lever Action

The Marlin 1895 SBL Lever Action rifle is a powerful and versatile firearm suitable for hunting, target shooting, and personal defense. Featuring a classic lever-action design, it offers smooth operation and reliable performance in various shooting conditions.

Key features:

  • Caliber: .45-70 Government
  • Action: Lever action
  • Capacity: 5+1 rounds
  • Barrel Length: 18.5 inches
  • Weight: Approximately 8 pounds
  • Stock: Black/gray laminated hardwood stock with pistol grip
  • Sights: XS Lever Rail with ghost ring sights and scout scope mount
  • Finish: Matte stainless steel metal finish

The Marlin 1895 SBL combines classic styling with modern features to deliver a reliable and accurate rifle suitable for a wide range of shooting applications. Whether you're hunting big game or enjoying a day at the range, the Marlin 1895 SBL is sure to impress.
