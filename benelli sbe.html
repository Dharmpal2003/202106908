Benelli SBE

The Benelli Super Black Eagle (SBE) is a semi-automatic shotgun renowned for its reliability, versatility, and performance in various shooting conditions. It features a gas-operated action system and a Comfortech recoil reduction system, providing smooth and comfortable shooting experiences.

Key features of the Benelli SBE include:

  • Caliber: 12-gauge
  • Action: Semi-automatic, gas-operated
  • Capacity: Varies depending on model and shell size
  • Barrel Length: 24-28 inches
  • Weight: Varies depending on configuration
  • Stock: Synthetic or walnut options available
  • Finish: Matte or camo finishes available
  • Chokes: Interchangeable choke system
  • Usage: Suitable for hunting, competitive shooting, and recreational shooting
