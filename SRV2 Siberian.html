SRV 2 Siberian Rifle

SRV2 Siberian

The SRV2 Siberian is a precision rifle known for its long-range accuracy and exceptional performance in various shooting scenarios. Designed for precision shooting and tactical applications, it offers advanced features and reliability.

Key features:

  • Caliber: .308 Winchester, 6.5 Creedmoor, .300 Winchester Magnum
  • Action: Bolt action
  • Capacity: Varies depending on magazine configuration
  • Barrel Length: 20-24 inches
  • Weight: Approximately 12-15 pounds
  • Stock: Adjustable precision stock with customizable cheek rest and length of pull
  • Trigger: Adjustable match-grade trigger for crisp and consistent pull
  • Optics: Picatinny rail for mounting optics and accessories
  • Finish: Durable and corrosion-resistant coating

The SRV2 Siberian rifle is engineered to meet the demands of precision shooters and long-range enthusiasts. With its superior accuracy and rugged construction, it excels in both competition and field use.
