CVA Optima

The CVA Optima is a break-action muzzleloader rifle designed for hunters and shooters who appreciate traditional firearms and the challenge of black powder shooting.

This rifle is known for its accuracy and reliability, making it a popular choice among muzzleloader enthusiasts. It features a break-open action for easy loading and cleaning, as well as a durable stainless steel barrel for consistent performance.

With its adjustable sights or scope mounts, the CVA Optima offers versatility in aiming options, allowing shooters to customize their setup according to their preferences and shooting conditions.

Key features:

  • Caliber: .50 caliber (other calibers may be available)
  • Action: Break-action, muzzleloader
  • Barrel Length: 24 to 28 inches (may vary depending on model)
  • Stock: Synthetic or wood options available
  • Ignition: 209 primer ignition system
  • Sights: Adjustable fiber optic sights or scope mounts
  • Finish: Stainless steel or blued finish
  • Accuracy: Sub-MOA accuracy with proper load development
