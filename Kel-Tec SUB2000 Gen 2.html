Kel-Tec SUB2000 Gen 2

About Kel-Tec SUB2000 Gen 2

The Kel-Tec SUB2000 Gen 2 is a semi-automatic rifle designed for ultimate convenience and portability. It's chambered in 9mm or .40 caliber and features a foldable design that makes it incredibly compact, perfect for backpacking trips and situations where space is limited. The rifle is adjustable and can easily accept most popular handgun magazines, offering versatility and practicality. Additionally, it can be quickly disassembled for cleaning or inspection without the need for tools, ensuring ease of maintenance.

Whether you're a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or someone looking for a reliable and compact firearm for personal defense, the Kel-Tec SUB2000 Gen 2 offers exceptional performance and convenience in a compact package.

Key Features:

  • Caliber Options: Available in 9mm or .40 caliber
  • Magazine Compatibility: Accepts most popular handgun magazines
  • Adjustable and Foldable Design: Convenient for storage and transport
  • Disassembly Without Tools: Easy maintenance
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for recreational shooting, home defense, and more

The Kel-Tec SUB2000 Gen 2 is a practical and versatile semi-automatic rifle, offering compact size, magazine compatibility, and ease of maintenance.
